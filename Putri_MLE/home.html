Major

The State Polytechnic of Malang organizes the Bachelor of Applied Science program education or what is often referred to as the Diploma IV program. The study period for the Bachelor or Diploma IV program is 4 years or 8 semesters, with details of 7 semesters of studying on campus and the last semester being used for Field Work Practices and working on Thesis.
Graduates of the Bachelor program at the State Polytechnic of Malang are S.ST (Bachelor of Applied Sciences). The S.ST degree will be obtained after the student has completed the load of 5,776 hours which is equivalent to a maximum of 160 credits. The Bachelor's program at the Polytechnic is equivalent to the Bachelor's program held at a university or institute of technology, but with a different amount of comparison between theory and practice. The Undergraduate Program at the Polytechnic is organized with a composition of about 45% theory and 55% practice. So that graduates from the Malang State Polytechnic will be more skilled and have better mastery in the field of practice.
